Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

NewsMood is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Data Collection

This app does not require any account login. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Sentiment Analysis API

When you search for a keyword, the app sends anonymized news summaries to a third-party API (Hugging Face) for sentiment analysis. No user information is included in these requests.

3. News Data

The app pulls public news headlines and summaries from third-party APIs such as NewsAPI, GNews, and Currents. No user data is sent during this process.

4. Local Data

Search history and sentiment scores are saved locally on your device. You can delete the app at any time to remove this data.
